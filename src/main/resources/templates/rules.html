Knockout stage predictions

Enter picks on Knockout. Predict the score after regular time (90′) only — not extra time.

  • If your predicted 90′ score is not a draw, the leading team advances automatically.
  • If your predicted 90′ score is a draw, also pick Penalty shootout? (Yes/No) and who advances (the team that wins after extra time / penalties in the real match).
  • Predictions lock at kickoff (UTC), same as group stage.

PickPointsNotes
Correct Penalty shootout? (Yes / No) +1 Only if you predicted a draw at 90′. Compares to whether the real match went to penalties after extra time.
Correct team to advance +2 Only when the real match is level at 90′. Wrong advancer = 0.

Example: you pick 1–1, penalties Yes, Brazil advances. Actual 1–1, penalties Yes, Brazil advances → 5 (exact) + 1 (penalties) + 2 (advancer) = 8 base points, then × round multiplier (e.g. ×1.5 in a quarter-final → 12). If actual is 2–1, penalty and advancer picks do not score — only the 90′ score table applies.
