Knockout stage predictions

Enter picks on Knockout — My Predictions. Predict the score after regular time (90′) only — not extra time.

  • If your predicted 90′ score is not a draw, the leading team advances automatically.
  • If your predicted 90′ score is a draw, also pick Penalty shootout? (Yes/No) and who advances (the team that wins after extra time / penalties in the real match).
  • Predictions lock at kickoff (UTC), same as group stage.

Tiebreakers (same total points)

If two or more players have the same total on the Leaderboard, rank is decided in this order:

  1. Higher success rate % on match score predictions — correct win/draw/loss outcomes ÷ all scored matches (shown under each player on the leaderboard)
  2. Correct champion pick — only after admin enters the official final result
  3. Display name (alphabetical)

Example: both players have 85 pts. Player A has 52% success rate and picked the champion correctly; Player B has 48% and did not → Player A ranks higher.

  • Group-stage and knockout match score predictions lock at kickoff (UTC).
  • Knockout picks open when both teams are confirmed for that match.
  • Group 1st/2nd predictions lock before each group's first match.
  • Final prediction locks at tournament kickoff (first group-stage match).
  • No negative points.
  • Admin enters official 90′ scores, whether a knockout match went to penalties, group results, and the final result after play ends.
