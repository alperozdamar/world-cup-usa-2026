Max 15 points if both places are exact. Locks at tournament kickoff.

Knockout stage predictions

Enter picks on Knockout — My Predictions. Predict the score after regular time (90′) only.

  • If your 90′ pick is not a draw, the leading team advances automatically.
  • If your 90′ pick is a draw, also pick Penalty shootout? and who advances.
  • Locks at kickoff (UTC).

Knockout extras (added on top of 90′ base)

PickPtsWhen it applies
Correct 90′ outcome (you picked a winner, not a draw) +1 Implicit advancer bonus — you called the team that wins at 90′.
Correct Penalty shootout? +1 You predicted a draw at 90′ and the real match was level at 90′.
Correct advancer (you predicted a draw, 90′ was a draw) +2 Right team after extra time or penalties.
Correct advancer (you predicted a draw, 90′ had a winner) +1 Wrong 90′ scoreline but right team to go through. Wrong advancer = 0.

Tiebreakers
  1. Higher success rate % on match predictions
  2. Correct champion pick (after final result entered)
  3. Display name (A–Z)
  • No negative points.
  • Admin enters 90′ scores, penalty shootout (Yes/No) on draws, group results, and final result.
